Great British Menu Starter. - Individuality

This dish is simplistic elegance. No surprise ingredients or molecular cooking techniques. Just a superb array of the freshest ingredients we have today. this if for you who have a discerning palate and still like the simply things in life, like carrots!

A Great British Menu Fish Course. Unique.

The theme is to create dishes that will be nostalgic with the war veterans, yet modern enough to grace any menu. The only extravagance on this plate was cooking the potatoes in saffron water. After that, all the parts are just humble vegetables we can easily get a hold of. I can’t recommend this dish enough, there is a lot of last minute cooking to get all the constituents to come together. However, once they’re on the plate, a superb dinner will be had by all. The beetroot powder is a revelation, extremely sweet but not in a sugary/dessert way. It compliments everything on the dish..

Mushroom and Fennel Crusted Pork Fillet, Beetroot Gnocchi and Cabbage GratinIntimate.

The secret to the taste of this dish is simply fennel seed and dried mushrooms blitzed to a powder. Added with some beetroot gnocchi. These gnocchis are quite delicate as they’re made with ricotta rather than potato. Then to top it off, bechamel flavoured with mustard that’s stirred into the cabbage and topped with breadcrumbs and parmesan..

Shoulder and fillet of lambSatisfaction.

This dish of slow cooked shoulder of lamb and fillet is so straight forward to do. It looks great and can choose between pork or beef. The shoulder is braised for a few hours, the meat is then forked apart and wrapped tightly in cling film, unleashing Greatness.
